How We Calculate Match Scores

Your personalized match score is calculated using a 4-dimensional algorithm designed to find cards that fit your spending habits, personality, and financial goals.

trending_up

Incremental Utility (45%)

We analyze how much extra value this card adds compared to what you already have in your wallet. If a card earns 4x on dining but you already have a 4x card, it scores lower than a card that fills a gap.

psychology

Personality Alignment (35%)

Scores how well the card fits your Financial Archetype. Does it match your preferred ecosystems (e.g., Apple, Travel) and strategy (e.g., Cash Back vs. Points)?

card_giftcard

Sign-Up Bonus ROI (15%)

Measures the net value of the welcome offer (Bonus - Fee) relative to the spending required. High bonuses with low fees score highest.

account_balance_wallet

Fee & Strategy Affinity (5%)

Checks if the card's annual fee aligns with your typical spending comfort zone. We penalize cards with fees significantly higher than your average.

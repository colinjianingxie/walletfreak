Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to link my bank account?

No, WalletFreak is a manual tracking tool. You simply tell us which cards you have, and we populate the benefits automatically. This ensures maximum security and privacy.

Does it track specific transactions?

Not directly. We focus on benefit utilization (e.g., "Did I use my monthly Uber credit?"). You manually log usage or mark benefits as complete.

Is it free?

Yes! WalletFreak is currently in open beta and completely free to use. We may introduce premium power-user features in the future.

Which issuers do you support?

We support all major US issuers including Chase, American Express, Capital One, Citi, Wells Fargo, Discover, and Bank of America.

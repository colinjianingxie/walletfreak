The Logic Behind The Rank

paid Net Value

We convert points into cash values (e.g., 1.0¢–1.5¢ per point) to determine the Total Dollar Return. This includes the Signup Bonus plus rewards earned on your planned spend.

(Bonus Value + Rewards on Spend) - Annual Fee

fact_check Feasibility Check

We verify you can meet the bonus requirement comfortably. The card's monthly burn rate must not exceed your own monthly spending pace.

(Card Spend Req / Signup Months) ≤ (Your Total Spend / Your Timeframe)

leaderboard Ranking Logic

Cards are ranked by highest **Net Value** (Total Profit). Ties are broken by **ROI** (Return on Spend). We prioritize putting the most money in your pocket over abstract efficiency.
